HelpOutPH is a portal for volunteers.

We bridge the gap between volunteers and disaster victims.

Here, as a volunteer, you can:
  • send in reports for people who need rescuing
  • see where rescue teams have been deployed
  • see where you can go to help in relief efforts
  • view causes we support, or add your own

Know someone who needs rescuing?

Enter details here and we'll relay to rescue teams. Please be as specific as you can, include:
  1. number of people in need of rescue
  2. level of flood in area (knee, waist, shoulder, etc)
  3. any info that might help rescuers
